Recipe for making Chicken Tandoori

Description:

Here we will be talking about how to make sizzling chicken tandoori. Making chicekn tandoori is quite straight forward and simple. All we need are the right ingredients and the right technique.

Ingredients:

  • A tandoor or a regular bon fire
  • Black salt
  • Chillies
  • Soy sauce
  • Lemons
  • And a chicken obviously

Steps:

  1. Set up the freshly cut chicken
  2. Inject in soy sauce and lemon juice into it as well as some chillies, spices and salt
  3. Set up a bon fire and place your freshly prepared chicken onto it
  4. Next let the chicken roast on there for 10-15 minutes on one side and then flip the chicken onto the next side
  5. Follow step 3 until the chicken is throughly cooked

Result:

Following these steps and ingredients will lead to you enjoying a freshly made sizzling chicken tandoori. Bonus tip keep a sprite in hand while enjoying your well earned delicasy.

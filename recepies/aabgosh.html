Recipe for making Kahmir's famous dish "Aab-Gosh"

Description

So in this post I will cover the entire recipe and ingredients required to make aab-gosh. Making aab gosh is a time consuming but fun process. Knowing how to make it can really help you in elevating your taste buds. So lets go.

Ingredients:

  • Beef or Mutton
  • Milk
  • Spices
  • Ghee
  • Table Salt and Sugar

Steps:

  1. Chop off the meat in desired chunks
  2. Fry it in ghee and spices for half an hour or until it is throughly cooked
  3. Add milk to the meat and let it boil
  4. After that add a fair amount of sugar to the meat
  5. Add some water and a bit more milk to the mixture until it boils.

Result:

You get a mouth watering tender and sweet meat dish which you can enjoy whenver you like.
